Better Voice

Private, on-device dictation and meeting notes for macOS. Your voice never leaves your Mac.

Download for macOS Apple Silicon · macOS 26+ · Free & open source on GitHub

Dictate anywhere

Hold your hotkey, speak, and the polished text is typed at your cursor — in any app.

Meeting transcripts

Record a meeting to get a speaker-labeled transcript and an AI summary, saved as Markdown.

Fully on-device

Transcription and summarization run locally against your own model server. Nothing is uploaded.

Knows your world

Optional personal context helps it spell names, jargon, and acronyms the way you do.

Stays out of the way

Lives in the menu bar with a global hotkey. Auto-updates in place — just restart to update.

Yours to shape

Point it at Ollama or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint and pick the models you prefer.

Getting started

  1. Download the latest .dmg and drag Better Voice to Applications.
  2. First launch is blocked by Gatekeeper — run xattr -cr /Applications/BetterVoice.app, then open it.
  3. The welcome screen walks you through permissions, your hotkey, and personal context.
  4. Press your hotkey to dictate, or start a meeting from the menu bar. That's it.

Privacy

Better Voice is built to keep your audio and text on your own machine. Recordings are transcribed on-device, and polishing/summarization talk only to the local (or self-hosted) model endpoint you configure. There are no analytics and no cloud accounts.
